Characters: Vincent

Description

Vincent is the creator and current leader of the monster hunter organisation. He's a man with brown hair that has a blue tint to it. His left eye seems to droop down a bit while his right eye is normal. He's a little taller than average height. Vincent is mostly seen as really nice by his employees but in private he has a tendency to spiral into suspicion about them. He often tries to be understanding when they make mistakes but is easily annoyed by their incopetence sometimes. When Vincent is upset he gets watery and starts to drip.

Backstory

He was born into a military (noble) family that despised weakness, he and his twin brother (James) were from a young age raised to be strong. He and his brother had a good relationship despite him always being “stronger”/better and he used to cover for his brother when he did something that their parents considered to be weak. As they got older their parents tried to arrange marriages for them. At age 16 they managed to find a wife for his brother. Then during the fourth meeting she awakened? as a monster upon which she killed both her and his entire family and took his right eye too. Seeking revenge he hunted and killed monsters and eventually was given his power by the gods as wished by the god of the sea. Seeing an opportunity he founded an organisation dedicated to finding and killing monsters which later grew to be huge. At this point his organisation is partially funded by the government.

Relationships and opinions

Ash

Ash is Vincent's best friend and the only person he actually trusts. They live together and are, despite them fighting a lot, still friends. Vincent likes Ash aside from his tendency to keep secrets from him and "not clean, ever".

The hunters

Although Vincent appriciates the work they do and is perceived as very nice by them, he is still very suspicious of them at the slightest mistake. In other words, he does not like them and believes they are actively planning his downfall.

Mr president

It's hard not to trust Mr predident. He is a cat after all and Vincent loves him more than anyone in the world. Even though he can be annoying sometimes

Other opinions

Vincent is very against the idea of establishing an HR for his organisation. He has stated that he's "not a very big fan of helping his employees actively plan his downfall."

Characters: Layla

Description

Layla is one of the monster hunters. Her job, other than hunting monsters is to gather intel from civilians who have encountered monsters. She is good at her job. She is a tall woman with liquidy purple hair and purple eyes. She is very nice to the people around her but she hangs around Emma a lot which makes others hesitate to approach her first. She has a secret.

Backstory

Relationships and opinions

Emma

Layla really likes Emma as a friend despite her mean and sometimes violent nature. She finds her being mean kinda cute since she long realised that she doesn't really mean it. She also pities Emma due to her bad upbringing and wants to help her work out her issues.

Samantha

Layla really likes Samantha due to her unwavering kindness. She finds her obsession with Lillian funny and really enjoys listening to Samantha talk about her various antics.

Sasha

Layla has seen Sasha hanging around Emma sometimes and finds it interesting. People don't really hang around Emma and Layla is happy that she's making some other friends but she's also worried that Sasha has ulterior motives or that Emma dislikes her.

Vincent

To others within the monster hunters Layla likes Vincent but to those outside of the monster hunters, she claims to hate him. In reality she's really conflicted about liking him due to her boss disliking him.

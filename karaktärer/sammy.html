Backstory

Her parents dissapeared at a young age upon which she was taken in by the monster hunters and trained to fight monsters. Since she had no power at the time, she wasn't sent out on missions until she got one at a later age. This led to her being able to make friends outside of the monster hunters and also why she met Sasha. She's the reason that a lot of good hunters have been hired and she's the best scout they currently have.

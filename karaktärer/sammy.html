Characters: Samantha

Description

Samantha is one of the monster hunters. Her job, apart from hunting monsters is to clean the organisation and clean the locations after monsters have been killed. She's also very good at scouting out people to join the monster hunters. Samantha is tall with wavy hair that has a blue tint to it and green eyes. She has a lot of freckles. Samantha is very positive and happy to the point that some of the other hunters find it weird sometimes. Altough she is overy positive sometimes, she's still very easy to talk to.

Backstory

Her parents dissapeared at a young age upon which she was taken in by the monster hunters and trained to fight monsters. Since she had no power at the time, she wasn't sent out on missions until she got one at a later age. This led to her being able to make friends outside of the monster hunters and also why she met Sasha. She's still looking for her parents. She's also the reason that a lot of good hunters have been hired and she's the best scout they currently have.

Relationships and opinions

Sasha

Sasha is Samantha's best friend. They met in school and Samantha is really happy to be friends with someone so amazing even though she asks her to do weird things sometimes. She feels bad that Sasha "seems to have a hard time with life". She finds Sasha funny.

Lillian

Samantha is infatuated with Lillian even though Lillian doesn't really seem to feel the same. This is not very obvious to Samantha after having been friends with Emma for a long time. Still, she tries her best to not make Lillian uncomfortable or weirded out and Lillian still talks to her.

Emma

Samantha likes Emma. She thinks that Emma is a really good listener even though she doesn't say much. She does think that Emma needs to come out of her shell and make other friends though.

Moe

Moe is like a father figure to Samantha. He practically raised her and Samantha really appreciates it. She's glad that he does't find her too weird.

Characters: Jack

Description

Jack is one of the monster hunters. His job, other than hunting monsters is to interrogate the monsters they manage to capture. Jack is average height and has dark green hair and pink eyes. He is blind in his left eye and has a visible scar over it. He is often very tired but has gotten good at hiding it. He sometimes wears red eyeliner because he thinks it looks really cool. The other hunters think that he's either annoying or a bit of an ass since he pushes people away when they try to get to know him.

Backstory

Jack grew up poor and was always busy with work or school when he grew up, leading him to not making any friends. When he found out about his power he gave no thought to joining the monster hunters. He still works multiple jobs to provide for his family and has still not made any real friends.

Relationships and opinions

Jasmine

Jack comes to the infirmary often. He tells himself that it's because he wants to help but in reality he just wants to talk to Jasmine. He, despite being afraid of admitting it, really likes her.

Other hunters

He likes the other hunters but is very suspicious that they have ulterior motives when they talk to him.

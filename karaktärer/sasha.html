Characters: Sasha

Description

Sasha is one of the monster hunters. Her job, apart from hunting monsters is to help in the infirmary. She is on the taller side and is strikingly beautiful. Her ginger hair and green eyes could leave people looking at her face for hours and she hates it. Although she acts polite and nice, she actively searches out insults which comes off as weird to the other hunters. Other than that she is very well liked but is only close to a select few.

Backstory

Sasha was always smart, effortlessly good at anything she tried. She got praised by everyone around her. People looked up to her and it felt like a dream but eventually it grew boring and annoying for Sasha and she began trying to fail and finding things she was bad at but never succeeded. Eventually she was given a gift, a power from the gods, she hated it though and after she was recruited by the monster hunters by her childhood friend Samantha, she began pretending that her power was weaker than it actually was.

Relationships and opinions

Samantha

Samantha is Sasha's best friend. She really enjoys how Samantha doesn't "act like she's special" or question her too much. They've been friends since they were children and Sasha feels like she's the only person that gets her. Sasha enjoys venting her feelings to Samantha.

Jonas

Sasha loves her brother even though they aren't close but she thinks he needs to learn to talk to people. She doesn't blame him for his lack of social skills though, instead blaming her parents. Sasha pities Jonas.

Emma

Sasha really likes Emma. She wants to learn more about her and also wants to be "near her always" despite Emma punching her in the face the first time they met. Sasha loves the fact that Emma hates her.

Ash

Sasha is curious about Ash and his relationship with Vincent. She also thinks he's pretty cool.

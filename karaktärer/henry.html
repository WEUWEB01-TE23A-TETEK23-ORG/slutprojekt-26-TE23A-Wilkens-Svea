Characters: Henry

Description

Henry is one of the monster hunters, despite being young. His job, other than hunting monsters is to interrogate the monsters that they manage to capture. He is short with greasy, messy hair that he thinks looks very good (it does not). He is very open about the things he likes but speaks in shorter sentences. He is not the smartest cookie and the other hunters used to see him as a son but now he's just kind of there.

Backstory

Abandoned by his parents and later found by Joffrey at a young age, Joffrey is basically his father figure. He later joined the monster hunters because Joffrey refused to join without him even though they didn’t want him. He also joined a band at some point where he has most of his friends.

Relationships and opinions

Joffrey

Joffrey is his adoptive father and also the reason he's in the monster huners. Henry likes Joffrey but doesn't talk about him unless asked.

Jonas

Jonas is Henry's best friend. He thinks that Jonas is really fun to be around and likes to force him into wacky shenanigans.

Nora

Nora his Henry's boss and he thinks that she's harsh toward him which makes him like to disobey her. He thinks it's funny how frustrated she gets with him.

Flora

Flora is Henry's classmate and also in the same band as him. She's really harsh toward him which makes him sad. People think that they're a couple, they are not.

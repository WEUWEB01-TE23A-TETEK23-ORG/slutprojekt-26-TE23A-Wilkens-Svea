Characters: Nora

Description

Nora is the boss of one of the various mosnter hunter locations. She is average height but likes to wear platform boots to appear taller. She is legally considered blind and has a tendency to accidentally walk into walls due to horrible depth perception. Nora is very strict and runs her location very well. Despite what one might think she is also well liked among her hunters and they believe their location to be the best thanks to her.

Backstory

She was born into a chaotic household and had many siblings. From a young age she resented the chaos and the fact that her parents never cared about it. Her eyesight also worsened as she got older causing her to get more care from her parents which she hated. When she eventually joined the monster hunters she could still use glasses but by the time she became the boss of her location she'd stopped using them because they barely helped. She accepted the job as the boss of the location because she liked having control and the ability to fix the location which she belived to be run horribly.

Relationships and opinions

Jerry

Nora doesn't like Jerry and thinks he is too unserious. But she's also conflicted about disliking him since she belives it's too cliché

Vincent

Nora has a lot of respect for Vincent but she also thinks that the way he runs the monster hunters has some issues. Therefore, the only rule she's ever broken in her life has been to make some changes to how her location of the monster hunters is run.

Henry

Nora does not like Henry because he is very messy and unorganised. She also believes that he is far too young to be a monster hunter.

Ash

Although Nora has barely talked to Ash, she belives him to be unworthy of running a monster hunter location, especially the main one.

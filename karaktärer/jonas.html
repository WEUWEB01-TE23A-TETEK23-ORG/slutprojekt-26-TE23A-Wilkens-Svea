Backstory

Jonas was never the favorite child. His sister was praised for everything she did and he was basically ignored but it rarely bothered him, it just meant that he had more time to himself! Because his sister outshined him in his early years he rarely got to interact with other people which made him a bit socially inept later in life and made him nervous around other people. Eventually he was gifted a power by the gods and joined the monster hunters, recommended by his sister Sasha.

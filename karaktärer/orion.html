Characters: Orion

Description

Orion is one of the monster hunters, altough due to his lack of a power, he doesn't hunt monsters. Instead, his job is to train new hunters since he himself has received training on how to use a power despite not having one. Orion looks basic. He's average height, has brown hair, brown eyes and an average build. There is nothing special with his appearance. His personality is also forgettable and the other hunters often forget who he is.

Backstory

Orion was always forgettable. Ever since he was a child, no one, apart from his parents have ever recognised him after their first meeting. That's why he was glad that he was chosen to be the next one to hold one of the family powers but even his grandpa, the current holder of the power forgot about him, instead giving it to his niece Star.

Relationships and opinions

Jonas

Jonas is Orion's student and also the only one of the monster hunters who actually remembers who he is. Orion is proud of Jonas but belives that he needs to make more friends and he often encourages Jonas to do so.

Moe

Moe is the other trainer at the main location of the monster hunters. Orion likes Moe and is happy that he even sometimes recognises him!

Star

Even though Star got the power instead of him, he doesn't resent them for it. Instead he feels bad at the backlash they received and hopes that they've found happiness and somwhere to belong after being kicked out from the family at such a young age.

Queen

Queen is Orion's dog. She's a brown border collie and Orion loves her more than anything. Orion is glad that Queen is well trained.

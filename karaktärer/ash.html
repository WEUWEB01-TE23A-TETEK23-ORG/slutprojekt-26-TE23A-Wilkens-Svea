Characters: Ash

Description

Ash is technically runs the main location of the monster hunters although his best friend Vincent does most of the work instead. Ash is a tall man with ashy blonde hair. His preferred weapon is a gun despite his terrible eyesight which is why he wears glasses. Ash is very rude to most people and is for the most part not liked by the other monster hunters. He is easily annoyed but often keeps it to himself unless he is close to the person.

Backstory

His parents died at a young age resulting in him being sent to an orphanage where he was quickly adopted by a family. His adoptive father liked to hunt and would often bring Ash along on his hunting trips which sparked his interest in guns and other weapons. One time Ash ran off on his own while hunting with his dad resulting in him getting lost and being alone in the forest for days until he was found. This resulted in him, despite refusing to admit it, being afraid of being alone

Relationships and opinions

Vincent

Vincent is Ash's best friend and Ash feels safe with him. They live together and are, despite them fighting a lot, still friends. Ash likes Vincent aside from his tendency to pester him to do things he doesn't want to do.

Emma

Ash and Emma don't talk much but despite Ash not admitting it, they are friends. They've bonded over their dislike for people and Ash enjoys her company and admires her ability to do her job.

Mr president

Ash has mixed feelings about Mr President. He finds him cute but also very annoying since he often begs to be pet or cuddled. Ash does think it's hilarious how Mr president likes him more than he likes Vincent.

Nora

Ash does not like Nora, stating her to be "Too bright".

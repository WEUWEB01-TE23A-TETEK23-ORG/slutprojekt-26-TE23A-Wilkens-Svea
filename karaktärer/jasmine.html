Characters: Jasmine

Description

Jasmine is a healer for the monster hunters. She is good at her job. Jasmine is a woman on the taller side with dark brown hair and black eyes that glimmer bright orange in light. She often complains about not being allowed to go on missions. She is well liked by the other hunters and people have a lot of respect for her.

Backstory

Jasmine always liked to fight. Being the youngest sibling, she wanted to prove herself to be strong and she did so by picking fights at every moment she could. When she eventually got her power and was scouted by the hunters she was happy that she could finally fight "for real". Sadly that was not the case. Instead Jasmine was put to work in the infirmary where she was the primary healer and was never allowed out on missions.

Relationships and opinions

Jack

Jasmine finds him strange but despite that she still really enjoys talking to him. She really likes him.

Samantha

Jasmine often wonders if Samantha is really stupid or just actually clumsy. Samantha often comes into the infirmary with various injuries and when asked she always gives the most stupid reason as to how she got hurt. Jasmine does find this amusing but also annoying at times.

Vincent

Jasmine's opinion on Vincent varies. On one hand, she finds him nice and appreciates his efforts as a leader but on the other hand, she gets really frustrated at the position he has put her in. She doesn't like how he treats her related to her job.

Miffy and Guy

She loves them and thinks that they are the cutest things that exist. She often calls them "perfect specimen".

Other hunters

Jasmine knows a lot about all the hunters and she loves it. It's probably the only thing she actually likes about her job.

About the world

The gods

After a few thousand years the gods realized that they could give pieces of their power to chosen humans (seed imagery could be cool to use for this). Because of their powers leaking from some humans, monsters were created.

The current gods are:

The goddess of love and the four seasons. She appears in front of people as the most beautiful person that person has ever seen. She is believed to be kind and loving like a mother and she likes to help people with their love problems. She is celebrated on 13/7 with a festival for those who celebrate her. Those who have her as a patron often have powers based in emotion. She is represented by a rose.

The god of change and destruction. He is said to be very different depending on his mood, which can change very quickly. Most often he is angry or cocky. He is often prayed to when a big change is happening in people's lives. He is celebrated on 31/12 through varying methods. Those who have him as a patron often have powers related to destruction or changing oneself or others. He is represented by the moonflower.

The goddess of the earth and creation. She is said to be caring, kind and feminine, being the symbol for femininity. She is celebrated on 2/7 by women around the world through parties dedicated to them. If you, as a woman, have your period on 2/7 it is said to be a good sign. Those who have her as a patron often have plant or earth related powers, in few cases she’s given art related powers. Almost all of those who have her as a patron are women. She is represented by the sunflower.

The god of the sea and knowledge. He is said to be withdrawn and frugal and rarely takes up the role as a patron. He is celebrated on the 15/10 but it’s often only celebrated by treating yourself or getting something small as a gift for a loved one. He is often worshipped by scholars. The lucky few who have him as a patron often have knowledge or water related powers. He is represented by the liverleaf flower.

The goddess of the sky and fate. She is said to be happy and energetic but also wise. She’s also said to love and cherish the sky as if it were her child. She is celebrated on 4/12 by a big fancy dinner for those who worship her. She is mainly worshipped by the Abadie family but has other worshippers as well. Those who have her as a patron often have powers related to the sky, space or fate. She is represented by a wood anemone flower.

The god of war and the people. He is said to be the only mortal who has become a god. Therefore he is mostly worshipped as the god of the people instead of the god of war which is the title he likes better. He is said to be heroic and honorable. His birthday is celebrated on the 24/12 and is a worldwide holiday. Those who have him as a patron often have powers that are combat related. He is represented by a dandelion.

The goddess of fortune and riches. She is said to be greedy and controlling, liking the thought of all the world's riches belonging to her. However she is also said to share pieces of her fortune to those she finds worthy. She is often prayed to by gamblers. Her birthday is celebrated on 18/6 by a grand golden parade. Those who have her as a patron often have game or luck based powers. She is represented by the golden rain flower.

The god of food and the weather. He is said to be kind and generous, often helping mortals and sharing his knowledge and power. When chefs come up with new recipes it is said that they’re possessed by him. He is mainly worshipped by farmers. He is celebrated on 30/8 with somewhat of a harvest festival where people mainly eat potatoes. Those who have him as a patron often have weather or eating related powers. He is represented by sweet peas flowers.

Creation

The world was created by the all powerful god who wanted to die. They thought they’d try giving away all their power therefore dying so they created a world and 100 000 people. They gave 100 people a mark and told all the people that if they had a mark by the end of six months they would get a piece of their power, they could obtain a mark by killing a person with a mark or if someone with a mark gave it to them and the more marks they had the bigger the piece they would get. After six months passed, the gods had been chosen and the remaining people without marks had their memories wiped and became the people of the world.
